Decision rule

For one-sample tests with known σ and n ≥ 30 (means) or np0 ≥ 10 and n(1 − p0) ≥ 10 (proportions). The proportion test uses SE = √(p0(1 − p0)/n); the proportion CI uses SE = √(p̂(1 − p̂)/n).
