GALTON BOARD — Binomial distribution → Normal approximation · Central Limit Theorem

Rows: 8 → 9 bins
Spawn: 6/s
Speed: Med
Each peg = fair Bernoulli trial (p=½). After n rows: bin k receives balls choosing k rights out of n. P(k) = C(n,k)/2ⁿ — exactly the binomial PMF. As n grows, the binomial → Normal(μ=n/2, σ=√(n/4)).
