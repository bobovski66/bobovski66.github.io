The statistical model sits as a Legendrian curve Lψ inside the three-dimensional contact manifold C₁ = Θ × M × ℝ. The Fisher–Rao metric is induced by pulling back the symmetric tensor ½(dθ ⊗ dη + dη ⊗ dθ) along the embedding; the potential coordinate z = ψ and the transverse Reeb direction live outside of the intrinsic information geometry.

Reeb flow shifts the normalization potential z by a constant; it is a strict contactomorphism and acts trivially on statistical content. The GL₁ action θ ↦ θ/c, η ↦ cη, z ↦ z is a strict contactomorphism sending Lψ to the Legendrian of the reparametrized family F̃ = cF.
Reading the picture
Drag on the 3D canvas to rotate. Scroll to zoom. The golden curve is Lψ; observe how the curve is tangent at every point to the teal contact plane, which tilts as η varies.
